The search server has the option to search complex database by many fields:
  • By complex:
    • PDB complex entry
    • Publish year: filter by range of years
    • Resolution: filter by range of resolution
    • Antibodiy: filter only the antibodies complexes
  • By proteins: (search at both proteins)
    • PDB protein entry
    • Accession number
    • Name: filter by the protein name
    • Length: filter by range of the length of both proteins
    • Scop family: filter complexes by specific proteins scop family
    • Publish year: filter by publication years
    • Identity: filter by the percentage identity of the seperate unbound proteins to the the complex proteins
