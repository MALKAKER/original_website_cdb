JCT Bioinformatics Center

The recent developments in biological technologies and computer science algorithms have enabled scientists to face new biological and medical challenges. The field of Bioinformatics supplies tools, approaches and solutions for coping with such challenges, specifically those involving high-throughput data. The JCT bioinformatics center focuses on a broad range of services in the fields of bioinformatics and biostatistics. Our center provides a variety of services including next-generation sequencing data analysis, database construction, machine learning analyses, bioinformatics consulting using computational methods and up-to-date software tools, in addition to scientific consulting services for development and execution of research projects.
