JCT Bioinformatics Center

The JCT bioinformatics center focuses on tailor made services in the fields of bioinformatics and biostatistics.
Our center provides a variety of services including:

  • Next-generation sequencing data analysis.
  • Machine learning analyses.
  • Database construction.
  • Software development.
  • Bioinformatics consulting using up-to-date software tools.

    Recent Projects

  • Finding molecular patterns distinguishing between sick and healthy patients based on statistical tools.
  • Analysis of cancerous histologiclal images.
  • Finding expression patterns of RNAseq - Collaboration with Dr. Michael Klutstein from the Hebrew Univesity.
  • Study of the association of hot spots and methylation - Collaboration with Dr. Michael Klutstein from the Hebrew Univesity.
